Beef Biryani

Beef biryani is a rich and flavorful dish that holds a special place in South Asian cuisine. It is made by layering tender, spiced beef with fragrant basmati rice, cooked together with aromatic ingredients like saffron, yogurt, and a blend of traditional spices. The irresistible aroma and delicious taste make it a favorite for gatherings and celebrations, loved by people of all ages.
