Mutton Biryani

Mutton biryani is a rich and indulgent dish known for its deep flavor and irresistible aroma. It is made by layering tender, slow-cooked mutton with fragrant basmati rice, infused with spices like cardamom, cloves, and saffron. The combination of juicy meat and aromatic rice creates a hearty and satisfying meal, making mutton biryani a favorite choice for special occasions and festive gatherings.
