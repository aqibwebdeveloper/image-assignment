Checken Karahi

Chicken karahi is a popular and flavorful Pakistani dish known for its rich taste and enticing aroma. It is made by cooking tender chicken pieces in a traditional karahi with tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and a blend of aromatic spices. The dish is cooked on high heat, giving it a thick, spicy gravy that perfectly coats the chicken, making it a favorite choice for both everyday meals and special occasions.
