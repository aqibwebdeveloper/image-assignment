Kabab Burger

Kabab wala burger is a flavorful and popular street-style dish loved for its spicy and juicy taste. It is made with a soft bun filled with a well-seasoned kebab patty, often accompanied by chutney, ketchup, onions, and sometimes a fried egg. The combination of smoky kebab flavor and fresh ingredients makes it a delicious and satisfying option for a quick meal.
